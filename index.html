About Us

Tripster is a newtourism organization formed to serve nature enthusiasts. We endeavor to providea diversion in one’s life by adding a spice of adventure. Tripster welcomes you to beautiful destinations of India torelish its exquisite landscapes and wildlife. We effectively cater to therequirements of Indian tourism sector.We have launched our activities with an adventurous tourthrough one of Karnataka’s thickest jungles.
